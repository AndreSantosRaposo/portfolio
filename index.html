Medical Application

An application for managing a doctor's patients. It keeps updated information on all patients (name, date of birth, ID card, phone, email) and their clinical records (blood pressure, weight, and height) in linked lists.

Features include:

  • Add and delete patients.
  • List all patients alphabetically.
  • List patients with high blood pressure above a given value.
  • Present all information about a specific patient.
  • Record blood pressure, weight, and height for a specific patient on a specific day.
  • Write patient and clinical record information to a file.
